Revolutionary Girl Utena

What's It About And What's Your Opinion On It?

Revolutionary Girl Utena! It's a magical girl/GL anime from the 90s. Here's the synopsis: "Utena is a tomboyish school girl who attends the prestigious Ohtori Academy. Her strong sense of chivalry pulls her into a series of duels with other members of the Student Council for the possession of the Rose Bride, a shy, mysterious girl named Anthy." It's my favorite because it's a very nuanced exploration of gender roles. I like how it breaks down the cycle of violence that patriarchy creates for women, while also showing possible ways to break free.
- Gita

Favorite Character

Utena Tenjou. I love her sense of chivalry and how she grows from her naivety over time.
- Gita
