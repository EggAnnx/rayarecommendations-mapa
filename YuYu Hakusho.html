Yu yu Hakusho

What's It About And What's Your Opinion On It?

Yu yu Hakusho! It's a series about some teenagers (one is a demon and the other one is a demon but still human?!?! human with a demon soul??) who beat up demons and bad ppl.

I think what I really liked about it was that it took the stereotypical shonen story but made it more unique and spread out. Although the series is tagged as an action, fighting series, I always found enjoying some of the "non-fighting scenes" as much as the fight scenes.

Its hard writing a shonen series with teen protagonists without making them act like fully matured adults or 2d characters who have nothing else in their mind aside from training and having a love interest. But the mangaka, Yoshihiro Togashi wrote the series really well that it was one of the first series where I would see my friends as the characters and imagine myself being part of the cast.

It's one of the first series I watched and it will definitely be my number 1 fav forever.
